Kasperia Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kasperia handles information when you use our mobile application and related wallet services. Kasperia is a non-custodial wallet designed for Kaspa-based transfers, payments, asset management, and related blockchain interactions.

By using Kasperia, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Non-Custodial Wallet

Kasperia is a non-custodial wallet. This means you remain in control of your wallet, private keys, seed phrases, passwords, and digital assets.

We do not hold, manage, recover, or access your private keys or seed phrases. You are responsible for keeping your wallet credentials secure.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Kasperia does not collect personal information from users. In particular, we do not collect:

  • Your name, phone number, email address, identity documents, or physical address.
  • Your private keys, seed phrases, passwords, or biometric data.
  • Your contacts, photos, files, messages, or other personal content stored on your device.
  • Your in-app behavior for advertising, profiling, or tracking purposes.

3. Blockchain Data

Blockchain transactions are public by nature. When you use Kasperia to view balances, transfer assets, connect to supported services, or check transaction status, the app may read publicly available blockchain data, such as wallet addresses, balances, transaction hashes, and network status.

This public blockchain data is not created by Kasperia and may remain permanently visible on the relevant blockchain network.

4. Local Data on Your Device

Certain wallet-related data may be stored locally on your device to allow the app to function properly. This may include wallet configuration, selected network settings, address labels, local preferences, and encrypted wallet data.

Kasperia does not upload your private keys or seed phrases to our servers. You may remove local app data by deleting the app or using device-level storage controls where available.

5. App Permissions

Kasperia may request limited device permissions only when needed for specific features. For example, camera access may be requested when scanning QR codes for wallet addresses or payment requests.

We do not use device permissions to collect unrelated personal data.

6. Third-Party Services and DApps

Kasperia may allow you to connect to decentralized applications, websites, blockchain nodes, explorers, or other third-party services. These services are not operated by Kasperia and may have their own privacy practices.

When you connect your wallet to a third-party DApp, the DApp may be able to view your public wallet address and request actions such as signing messages or submitting transactions. Always review requests carefully before approving them.

7. Analytics and Advertising

Kasperia does not use your personal information for advertising, personalized marketing, or user profiling. We do not sell user data.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of the app and local wallet environment. However, blockchain transactions are irreversible, and no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You should protect your device, back up your seed phrase securely, avoid phishing websites, and carefully verify all transaction details before confirming any action.

9. Children’s Privacy

Kasperia is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be made available within the app or on our official website. The updated date at the top of this page will indicate the latest revision.
